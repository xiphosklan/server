THE PRAETORIAN KORP

INTERNAL ENFORCEMENT

The Korp acts as the internal police force. They ensure the Phalanx remains pure, disciplined, and free from dissent.

INNER CIRCLE

They operate in the shadows, answering only to the Imperator. Their identities are often obscured to maintain authority.

THE BLADE

When standard discipline fails, the Korp is deployed to eliminate high-value threats or traitors to the Klan.
