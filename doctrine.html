OPERATING DOCTRINE

PROTOCOL 001 // HIERARCHY

The chain of command is absolute. Orders flow downwards. Dissent flows nowhere.

PROTOCOL 002 // COMMS

Microphones are mandatory during operations. Unnecessary noise in comms is a structural failure.

PROTOCOL 003 // ENGAGEMENT

We do not engage unless ordered. The Krypteia strikes when the blade is sharpest, not when the blood runs hottest.
